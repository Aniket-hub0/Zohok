About

  • Who are We?

    We are BrainwaveS Neurorehab Solutions. We aim to develop and provide complete Neuropsychological Rehabilitation solutions using cutting edge technology to address the cognitive, behavioural and motor deficits that result from injury to the brain.

  • What We do?

    We develop affordable solutions catering to patients suffering from post-stroke symptoms like paralysis and muscle movement. Our solutions aim to facilitate such patients throughout their rehabilitation process, making it as fun, easy and engaging as possible.

  • How we do It?

    We are using cutting edge technology like Virtual Reality and Brain-Computer Interfaces to address the cognitive, behavioural and motor deficits that result from injury to the brain. Our suite includes a range of products from fully immersive VR games customized for rehabilitation to motion-capture hardware modules.

  • Are We Commercial?

    We are not commercial yet, still in the design and development stage. We are backed by major organisations and experts from this field who are helping us make this product the finest of its kind.
